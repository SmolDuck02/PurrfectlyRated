#About

Project Purrfectly Rated

was crafted as a culmination of the team's coursework in Information Management 2 during their third year at Cebu Institute of Technology - University.

Developed using the Django web framework, Python, and vanilla JavaScript, the project follows the principles of a RESTful API. This platform caters to a user base that the team has affectionately dubbed as "Purrfects," offering them a space to share their experiences with various pet products.

The goal is to foster community learning and engagement by enabling Purrfects to articulate their insights, providing valuable perspectives for the wider community.
